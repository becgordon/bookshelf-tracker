Welcome to Shelf-Help!

Shelf-Help is a personal library tracker. Gone are the days of picking up used copies at your favorite bookstore only to realize you already had them at home. Users can search for books to add to their library as well as rate, sort, and categorize them. Can't decide what to read? The click of a button offers up a suggestion from the 'To Be Read' shelf. Users can label their current reads and share their library with others. Charts are available to visualize library statistics like 'Have Read Vs To Be Read'. Searched a book but don't know where to find it? A link is included in every search result to purchase from Bookshop.org, a website that focuses on putting profit back into the hands of independent bookstores.
