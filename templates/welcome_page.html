Welcome to Shelf-Help!

Shelf-Help is intended to help users track their physical book collection. It utilizes the Google Books API to search and store information about a user's books. It allows them to rate, categorize, and view statistics about their library. Users can view other users' libraries and add books to their own library from there. The site can offer up a book to read next from the user's To Be Read shelf and it offers the ability to share your current read.
